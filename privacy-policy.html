This privacy policy applies to the Simple Notes app (hereby referred to as \"Application\") for mobile devices. The Application is a note-taking app provided as a free service and is intended for use \"AS IS\".

The Application collects and stores only the notes you create, including note titles, content, and timestamps.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to outside parties.

You can delete your notes at any time directly within the Application.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop using the Application at any time by uninstalling it from your device.

To request deletion of your data from our servers, please contact us via email.
